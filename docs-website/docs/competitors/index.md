# Competitor Analysis

## Overview

This section provides comprehensive analysis of key competitors in the voice AI and business automation space, particularly focusing on New Zealand-based companies and those targeting similar markets.

## Analysis Framework

For each competitor, we analyze:

- **Business Model**: How they generate revenue and serve customers
- **Services & Features**: Core offerings and capabilities
- **Pricing Strategy**: Cost structure and market positioning
- **Technology Stack**: Technical infrastructure and integrations
- **Target Market**: Customer segments and geographic focus
- **Competitive Positioning**: Strengths, weaknesses, and market differentiation
- **Relation to SkyGuyver**: Direct comparison and competitive gaps
